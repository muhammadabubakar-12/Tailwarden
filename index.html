Introducing Tailwarden

A new way to uncover your costs

Tailwarden is an all-in-one tool that allows you to build your cloud assets inventory across all your cloud infrastructure in order to provide visibility into your costs and assist you in making informed decisions.

Dive deep into the intricate layers of your cloud. Know what you have, where it's located, and how it's tagged.

One platform, every major cloud

Tailwarden is fully cloud-agnostic, seamlessly integrating with major providers like AWS, GCP, Azure, OVH, and DigitalOcean.

Non-invasive account access

Tailwarden uses "read-only" access to your infrastructure. This ensures we can't access your database, view private data, or act on your infrastructure.

No idle resources, complete visibility

With Tailwarden's Virtual Tags, gain complete visibility into your cloud assets. Ensure every resource is properly tagged, driving optimal utilization and eliminating idle assets.

Built on open-source

Tailwarden is based on the open source project Komiser, used by +3M developers today.
